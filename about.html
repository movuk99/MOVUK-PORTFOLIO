About

Matías Marcin.

Field notes

I was born and raised in Mexico City and am currently studying Communications and Digital Media at IE University in Madrid, Spain. My creative work began after moving to Europe and focuses primarily on videography and photography, with an emphasis on landscapes, nature, culture, and travel. While these are my main areas of focus, I see my practice as evolving — driven by curiosity, storytelling, and visual exploration.

Beyond audiovisual creation, I am deeply passionate about mountaineering. Through the IE Outdoors Club, I've had the opportunity to organize and lead a growing community while also shaping the club's brand identity and managing its social media presence.

OCMT is currently my most recent and significant project. Through it, I've built an international community focused on entering the world of mountaineering, while also developing a creative hub dedicated to mountain sports content.

I also serve as an officer in the university's Photography Club, where I organize events and lead workshops focused on photography and audiovisual creation. In addition, I hold a position in Student Government, where I collaborate on initiatives that strengthen student engagement, improve transparency, and develop projects that connect students, faculty, and the broader community.
