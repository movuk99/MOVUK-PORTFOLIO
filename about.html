About

Behind the work: Matías Marcín.

Field notes

Born and raised in Mexico City, currently pursuing a degree in Communications and Digital Media at IE University in Madrid, Spain, with studies at ESPM University in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. My creative work began after moving to Europe and focuses primarily on videography and photography, with an emphasis on landscapes, nature, culture, and travel. While these are my main areas of focus, I see my practice as evolving — driven by curiosity, storytelling, and visual exploration.

Beyond audiovisual creation, I am deeply passionate about mountaineering and outdoor sports. As president of the IE Outdoors Club, I've had the opportunity to organize and lead a growing community while also shaping the club's brand identity and managing its social media presence.

OCMT is currently my most recent and significant project. Through it, I've built an international community focused on entering the world of mountaineering, while also developing a creative hub dedicated to mountain sports content.

Apart from these passions, I find purpose in travelling the world and capturing moments that help me comprehend how small we are in this immense world, which in a way makes the human experience a unique thing. In addition, I've held positions and interned in plenty of different projects and groups that have allowed me to strengthen my organization skills, get myself into real world work experience, work under pressure, and deliver results.
